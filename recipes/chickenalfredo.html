Chicken Alfredo

Description

This is chicken alfredo. It is my favorite pasta dish. I enjoy eating it for every meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of chicken
  • 1 cup of alfredo
  • Noodles of your choosing

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken to an internal temperature of 165 degrees
  2. Cook noodles until al dente
  3. Add alfredo sauce and chicken to noodles
